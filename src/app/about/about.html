My Story

From curiosity to career

It started in 2023, in a small room in Pampanga, with a cheap laptop and a lot of curiosity. I was 18, fresh out of high school, and had no idea what I wanted to do with my life. Then I discovered coding‚Äîand everything changed.

I remember building my first website: a terrible, ugly mess of broken HTML and mismatched colors. But when I opened it in a browser and saw something I'd actually created, I felt a rush I'd never experienced before. That moment hooked me. I knew this was what I wanted to do.

"The first website I built was terrible. But it was mine. And that mattered more than perfection."

I enrolled at Holy Angel University for BS Web Development, diving headfirst into everything: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, design principles, user experience. Late nights became my norm. Every bug fixed felt like a victory. Every new concept learned opened up new possibilities.

By my second year, I started taking on freelance clients. Small projects at first‚Äîa local coffee shop's website, a friend's portfolio. Each project taught me something new: how to communicate with clients, how to manage timelines, how to translate vague ideas into working code.

Today, 3 years later, I've worked on over 20 projects, collaborated with clients from around the world, and built a reputation for delivering quality work with attention to detail. But what drives me isn't the projects or the clients‚Äîit's still that same feeling I had when I saw my first website working. The magic of creation.
