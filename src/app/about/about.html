Professional Biography

I'm Smith Dainielle L. Romero, a passionate Full Stack Developer and 3rd-year IT student at Holy Angel University, specializing in MEAN and MEVN stack development. My journey into web development started with curiosity about how websites work, which quickly evolved into a deep passion for building complete, end-to-end applications.

Currently maintaining a Dean's Lister standing, I've developed expertise across the entire development stack - from designing responsive frontend interfaces with Angular and Vue.js to building robust backend systems with Node.js and Express, and managing data in both SQL and NoSQL databases.

What excites me most about full stack development is the ability to see a project through from concept to completion. I enjoy the challenge of understanding how all the pieces fit together - creating seamless user experiences while ensuring the underlying architecture is solid, scalable, and efficient.

I've built multiple projects that solve real problems, including the Tambayan App for HAU students to find study spots, a restaurant website for Geytway Unli Buffet, and various full-stack applications. Each project has strengthened my skills and reinforced my commitment to writing clean, maintainable code.
